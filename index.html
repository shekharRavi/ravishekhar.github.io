Ravi Shekhar

I am a Ph.D. Student at DISI, University of Trento in Trento, Italy, under the supervision of Raffaella Bernardi. During my Ph.D., I have amazing collaboration with Dialogue Modelling Group, University of Amsterdam lead by Raquel Fernández. I also collaborate with Barbara Plank from IT University of Copenhagen. I also collaborated with MHUG from University of Trento.

Prior to my Ph.D., I spent one year as a research assistant at University of Malaya under C. S. Chan. I received my M.S. (by Research) Degree with focus on Computer Vision from CVIT, IIIT Hyderabad under the supervision of C. V. Jawahar. Prior to that, I worked as Software Engineer for Accenture Services Pvt. Limited .

Research

My research lies at the intersection of machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing with an emphasis on learning Deep Neural Networks form the multi-modal data.
